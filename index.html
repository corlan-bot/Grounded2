Co-op Progression Roadmap

3-Player Strategic Plan — Grounded 2

Brookhollow Park  ·  All four phases

Early game Survive the first hours

Before splitting up, stick together and complete the tutorial story quests. The narrative naturally unlocks Workbench, Omni-Tool, and your first Ranger Station — the backbone of everything that follows.

Team role split
Player 1 — Warrior
Ladybug or Red Ant armor · club/sword · tank threats, clear the path · equip Meat Shield mutation
Player 2 — Ranger
Pebblet Spear + bow · scout ahead, pull enemies · equip Sharpshooter · handles Mite clearing
Player 3 — Gatherer
Focus on resource loops · Pebblet + Sprig + Plant Fiber · manage the Workbench · unlock new recipes first
Priority activities
  • 1
    Fix Omni-Tool key bindings immediately
    Default bindings cause accidental chopping and mount-triggering. Remap before exploring.
  • 2
    Scan everything at the Resource Analyzer
    Analyze from hand — no need to inventory first. Prioritize: Pebblet, Sprig, Plant Fiber, Mite parts, sap. Unlocks all early recipes.
  • 3
    Build base near a Ranger Station with water access
    Elevate it on a rock, root, or fence beam — keeps most bugs out. Link to Field Station for analyzer/surveyor access.
  • 4
    Farm Reliable Friend mutation early
    Switch to Whoa mode temporarily · have teammates down and revive each other repeatedly · dramatically cuts revive time for boss fights.
  • 5
    Collect every Milk Molar you spot
    Spend first molars on mutation slot upgrades (unlock slots 3 → 4 → 5). More mutations = massive power spike.

Mid game Establish dominance

With the base secured and Tier 1 weapons in hand, begin splitting the team for parallel progression. One player scouts new biomes while the others upgrade the base and craft mid-tier gear.

Refined team roles
Player 1 — Tank/Warrior
Upgrade to Ladybug Armor · wield club + shield · Barbarian mutation · leads dungeon runs and boss pulls
Player 2 — Ranger/Scout
Spider Stringer bow · Orb Weaver armor · marks threats, clears range · scouts biomes for Milk Molars
Player 3 — Rogue/Support
Mosquito Needle rapier · light armor · lifesteal sustain · manages Smithing Station upgrades and smoothie production
Priority activities
  • ★
    Unlock and progress the Egg Hunt quest
    Requires Optical Disc · unlocks the Buggy mount. Use Buggy to haul grass planks and weed stems — huge logistics upgrade for the team.
  • ★
    Upgrade Omni-Tool to Tier 2
    Requires Roach Chunks + Lingonberry Leather + Scorpion Stingers · unlocks access to mid-game gear tier including Snail Shell armour.
  • ⚒
    Build the Smithing Station
    Enables weapon upgrades. Collect Brittle Whetstones (Quartzite) and Brittle Plating (Marble) to level weapons to rank 5.
  • ⚒
    Upgrade Cookery · shift to smoothie production
    Smoothies stack and give bonus boosts. Stock the team with max health smoothies before every dungeon run. Far superior to roasted meat mid-game.
  • ⚒
    Save Optical Discs for the Science Shop
    Raw Science is the premium currency. Prioritize: mutation slot upgrades, Tier 2 Omni-Tool, key structure unlocks. Don't waste on cosmetics.

Late game Dominate the park

Tier 3 weapons, legendary drops, and boss preparation. The team should now be running coordinated dungeon clears with defined roles — tank pulls aggro, ranger burns from distance, rogue flanks and finishes.

Key activities
  • !
    Full team: complete all story main quests (~20 hrs)
    ~15 main quests, each under an hour. Add time for gear grinding before boss fights. Story unlocks new biomes and the Tier 3 gear pathway.
  • !
    Upgrade weapons past rank 5 with Sturdy Whetstones
    Collect Sturdy Whetstones + Sturdy Plating for ranks 6–9. Match damage type to enemy weakness: slashing vs bugs, smashing vs armoured insects.
  • ⚔
    Hunt and equip unique/legendary weapons
    Ice Sickles (best dagger), Crimson Avenger (sword + spider counter), Tick Macuahuitl (lifesteal), Glacial Sting (freeze control) — each hidden or boss-locked.
  • ⚔
    Mage variant: unlock Candy Staff / Heatwave
    Heat Wave staff — massive AOE fire damage for mob clearing. If Player 3 switches to Mage role, pair Butterfly Armor + Fire Ant Shield mutation.
  • ◈
    Explore all biomes: Frozen Wastelands, Cobblestone Labyrinths, Sewage Tunnels
    Each zone has biome-specific materials and hidden legendary drops. Always send the Ranger scout first with bow ready.

Endgame Conquer everything

All story quests complete, max-upgraded weapons in hand. Focus on clearing all bosses, securing all unique drops, expanding the base, and preparing for Winter 2025/2026 update content.

Endgame priority activities
  • ✦
    Max all 5 mutation slots per player — build 4 Loadouts each
    Create loadouts for: boss fights, mob clearing, exploration, and base defence. Swap rapidly without menu navigation.
  • ✦
    Upgrade Omni-Tool to Tier 3 (Winter update)
    Tier 3 locked until Winter update — stock required materials in advance: high-tier roach, lingonberry, and scorpion stinger variants.
  • ✦
    Clear all bosses with coordinated role execution
    Warrior holds aggro + parries · Ranger burns from safe distance · Rogue/Mage flanks for burst. Rotate resurrection duties using Reliable Friend.
  • ✦
    Full park exploration — find all Milk Molars
    Use the Resource Surveyor (unlocked after Oak Lab) to scan the map and track remaining collectibles. Molar income = permanent stat gains for the whole team.
  • →
    Prepare for Summer 2026 content update
    Obsidian roadmap confirms Summer 2026 expansions. Stock Science Discs and surplus resources so the team can immediately progress new content on release.
